Sour Cream Bundt Cake

  • Time: 15:00
  • Serves: 30 min
Ingredients Quantity
package yellow cake mix 1
teaspoons vanilla extract 2
cup sour cream 1

Step 1:Make the batter following the directions on the box, EXCEPT replace 2 teaspoons water with an equal amount of vanilla. Fold in sour cream. Bake according to directions given for baking a bundt cake. Cool on rack, place on serving plate, and dust with confectioners sugar.
